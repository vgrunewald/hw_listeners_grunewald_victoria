About Seattle

The Weather

Despite what people may think, it does not rain all the time here. Winters are normally mild and the summers are perfect for taking advantage of all the outdoor activities the area has to offer.

Food, Beer and Wine

The city is full of incredible restaurants with cuisines from around the world. Washington is the number one U.S. producer of premium wine and hops. Top wineries and craft breweries can be found throughout the greater Seattle area.
